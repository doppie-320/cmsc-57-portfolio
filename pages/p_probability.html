PROBABILITY
Probability, second to counting in terms of difficulty, was another subject I struggled really hard with. Most of my struggles was not the computation of the mathematical formulas, I could probably solve those accurately in mixed fractions or improper fraction values even without a scientific calculator, but the real difficulty for me was deriving those mathematical formulas, interpreting, evaluating, and structuring the approach to a tangible solution from phraseology in natural languages like english and context clues. But the most fun I had from this topic and statistics in general was during the Minecraft speedrunning scandals back in the pandemic where a lot of the mentioned topics arose to help prove an allegedly manipulated speedrun as invalid.
